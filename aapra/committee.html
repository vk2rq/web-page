Our Meetings...

The committee meetings are held at 7:30pm on the second Thursday of every month at AAPRA HQ, 59 Westbrook Ave, Wahroonga. In winter the meetings are accompanied by a roaring fire, and all year round coffee, tea and cakes are served at the conclusion of the meeting.

Meeting dates for 2003
23 Jan 23 Feb (Wyong) 13 Mar
10 Apr 8 May 1 Jun (AGM)
12 Jun 10 Jul 14 Aug

The Annual General Meeting is held at the same venue on one Sunday in May each year and is followed by a sausage sizzle.
