Packet Radio Software

This page lists the top packet radio software and is regularly updated to show the latest versions and links to shareware sites. Most of the titles listed are available on floppy disk and CD from AAPRA. Software supporting other digital modes of interest to radio amateurs are also shown and there are some useful utilities.
